# Welcome to My Blog

I've been meaning to start a blog for a while, and I'm finally doing it. My goal is to use this space to write about astrophysics, AI, and occasionally, music. 

## What to expect

Most posts will fall into one of a few categories:

- **Research updates** — highlights from recent papers on astrophysics and AI.
- **Explainers** — accessible write-ups on topics I find fascinating, aimed at a general scientifically curious audience.
- **Career & academia** — occasional thoughts on the academic research path, fellowships, writing, industry, and advice I wish I'd had earlier.

I'll write more about all of this soon. Thanks for stopping by.

— Tony
