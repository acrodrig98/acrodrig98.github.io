### AccDiskSpec: FU Ori Accretion Disk Spectral Modeling

**[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/acrodrig98/AccDiskSpec)**

A Bayesian spectral-fitting framework for modeling the multi-wavelength spectra of FU Ori accretion disks. The code enables inference of mass accretion rates and disk temperature profiles from broadband photometry and spectroscopy.

Associated paper: [Rodriguez et al. 2022, ApJ, 927, 144](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2022ApJ...927..144R/abstract)
